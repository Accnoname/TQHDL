AI Impact Jobs 2010-2025

A compact dashboard for the AI impact jobs analysis. Run the pipeline to refresh figures, then open this file to review all steps in one place.

Dataset: ai_impact_jobs_2010_2025.csv Outputs: reports/figures Scope: 2010-2025

Step 01 - Overview

Job postings by year, top industries, top countries, and seniority by region.

Step 02 - Univariate

AI intensity, automation risk distribution, adoption stage mix, and salary spread.

Step 03 - Bivariate

Salary by seniority, top industries by salary, and automation risk by industry.

Step 04 - Trends

AI mention rate, salary trends, automation risk trends, and reskilling needs.

Step 05 - Skills and Text

Top skills, top AI keywords, reskilling by industry, and displacement risk mix.

Step 06 - Strategic

Risk distribution by adoption stage and optional interactive world maps.

If the interactive maps exist, they will load below. Otherwise, run the step 06 script with Plotly installed to generate the HTML outputs.
Dashboard file: reports/dashboard.html. Update figures with python main.py when needed.
